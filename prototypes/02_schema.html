AI-Generated Summary

This table tracks every customer order on the Olist marketplace. Each row is one order, identified by order_id. It records which customer placed the order, the current order status (delivered, shipped, canceled), and four timestamps covering purchase, approval, carrier handoff, and delivery. This is the central fact table. Most other tables (order_items, payments, reviews) reference it through order_id. Use order_status to filter for completed vs. in-progress transactions. The gap between purchase_timestamp and delivered_timestamp gives you fulfillment time.
